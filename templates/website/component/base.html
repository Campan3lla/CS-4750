Office Hour Management

Created by Jonah Kim, Yuxi Yang, and Tammy Ngo

Description

This website allows for instructors (both professors and TAs) to create office hours, specifying information concerning the time, location, and class. They will also be able to view some statistical summaries pertaining to each class they are teaching or assisting in. On the other hand, students will be able to freely view available office hours corresponding to the classes they are registered to. Then, students will be able to submit questions relating to specific assignments for instructors to answer. Once answered, instructors will be able to close them once commented on.
